Planty

A complete brand identity for Planty, an innovative plant-sitting service. Developed strategy, branding assets & identity, and a final pitch video from concept to launch with Maria Gomez.

01

Brand Strategy

Established Planty's market position as a reliable, modern plant care service targeting busy professionals. Developed brand voice, core values, and messaging framework.

02

Visual Design System

Created a cohesive visual identity with a custom logo, colour palette, and typography. Designed 3+ logo concepts and refined to a final mark that conveys growth, care, and friendliness.

03

Digital Applications

Assisted Maria in designing a responsive Facebook page, website and mobile app interfaces showcasing services, pricing, and customer testimonials.

04

Marketing & Collateral

Created brand assets, including business cards, packaging and garden-related products.

Pitch Video

Professional video production showcasing Planty's brand story, service offering, and value proposition. Filmed and edited the entire result.
