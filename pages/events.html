Event Highlights

Goal

Create fast-paced, energetic videos highlighting the events to attract students to attend.

Strategy

Focus on the action parts of the events, using quick cuts to keep viewers engaged and help them imagine participating. Use energetic songs and keep the video length under a minute.

Outcome

Each video received over 4 thousand views, increased engagement on event posts, and encouraged more students to attend events.
